# **App Name**: BrokBuddy

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Lead Insights: Utilize generative AI as a tool to analyze market data and property attributes to generate predictive lead quality scores and provide actionable insights for Australian agents.
- Comprehensive Deal & Pipeline Tracking: Manage and track real estate deals through their lifecycle, including revenue forecasting and performance monitoring via a centralized dashboard.
- Client & Property Management: Maintain a secure database of client profiles and property listings, including communication history and associated documents.
- Agency & Team Oversight: Administer user roles and permissions for agents and staff, facilitating team collaboration and brokerage-level oversight.
- Automated Workflows & Notifications: Streamline agent tasks through automated workflows for follow-ups and custom notifications to ensure timely actions and reduce administrative friction.
- Secure Document Management: Store, organize, and secure important legal and property documents in the cloud with robust access controls and data protection.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: Champagne Gold (#DEBE6A), derived from a sophisticated warm yellow hue, will highlight Call-To-Action buttons and key interactive elements against the dark theme.
- Background color: Deep Charcoal (#2E2B27), a very dark, slightly warm grey, will provide a rich, luxurious base, ensuring elements stand out and are easy to read.
- Secondary accent color: Rich Terracotta (#B34D26), an analogous hue to the primary gold, will provide complementary depth and highlight less critical interactive components or progress indicators.
- Primary text color: Pearl White (#F0F0F0) for body text and headlines, offering crisp readability and a clean contrast against the dark background.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end feel in headlines, reflecting the elite system's aesthetic.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for modern readability and a touch of warmth in all body text, supporting the clarity required for managing complex information.
- The BrokBuddy logo will appear in a monochrome, all-white or all-silver version. All other icons will follow a sleek, modern line-art or glyph-style, appearing in monochromatic silver or gold accents to maintain the luxury aesthetic.
- The application will utilize a 'Bento Box' or modular grid layout, confined to a single viewport to ensure a non-scrollable experience for agents, maximizing information density.
- The layout is divided into a static left-side hero section for branding and core messaging, and an interactive right-side feature grid, ensuring optimal spatial organization.
- A sleek and minimal top navigation bar will contain essential branding and contact information without cluttering the primary view.
- Interactive feature modules will smoothly flip or fade to reveal their bulleted content on hover, maintaining a dynamic yet minimal user experience.
- The primary Call-To-Action button will subtly glow on hover, drawing attention as the focal point of the page.
